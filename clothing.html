About Elvin Threads

Our Story

Founded with a vision to empower women through elegant and comfortable fashion, Elvin Threads has been crafting beautiful dresses that blend sophistication with everyday comfort since 2018.

Our Founder Elvin, grew up surrounded by the rich textile traditions of her family. With a degree in Fashion Design from the prestigious Parsons School of Design, she combined her cultural heritage with contemporary fashion trends to create a unique brand identity.

Elvin Threads specializes in semi-formal and casual women's dresses that are perfect for any occasion - from important business meetings to casual weekend outings. Each piece is thoughtfully designed with attention to detail, quality fabrics, and flattering silhouettes that celebrate the modern woman's diverse lifestyle.

Today, Elvin Threads continues to grow while maintaining the personal touch and quality standards that Momina established. Our collections reflect her belief that every woman deserves to feel confident, beautiful, and comfortable in her own skin.
